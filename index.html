About Us

Viperbots

ViperBots mission is to provide students with opportunities to add depth to their learning through problem solving, teamwork, and skill building. This is done through mentors who have both technical and non-technical experience in design, construction, programming, and competition.

Gracious Professionalism

Gracious professionalism is an invaluable component of the ethos of FIRST Robotics. ViperBots Stryke as a whole seeks to embody knowledge, respect, and empathy in all of its actions. We learn from our successes as well as our failures. We also acknowledge the value of criticism, as well as praise. Ductos Exemplo, a Marine Corps principle meaning “lead by example,” inspires us to display self-reliance and actively help other robotics teams in our school and community.

Community Outreach

ViperBots sets a high standard for gracious professionalism by teaming up with local middle schools’ First Lego Leagues. We understand the importance of cultivating a strong interest in science and technology among young minds. Our elementary feeder schools also come to Vandegrift on field trips to see robotics demonstrations. Stryke members reach out to the feeder elementary and middle schools to provide technical support, guidance, and encouragement. Viperbots also plans to offer weeklong summer camps for elementary and middle school students and help them by serving as counselors and mentors.
